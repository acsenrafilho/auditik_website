APPS AND INTEGRATIONS
Microsoft Teams Integration
Microsoft (MS) Teams is now available one click away from WhatsApp chats with your favorite tools and features on a single screen. Tuvis instantly integrates the powerful features of MS Teams into WhatsApp Web, providing easy and direct access to day-to-day tasks without leaving the instant messaging screen.
Initiate instant Microsoft Teams video calls with individuals or groups
When integrating your MS Teams account into WhatsApp, you will be able to initiate a MS Teams meeting and automatically send the link of the meeting to your contact without leaving WhatsApp.
Schedule MS Teams meetings with contacts
When scheduling a meeting through the Tuvis’ panel on WhatsApp, you will be able to select MS Teams, and the link to the MS Teams meeting will be automatically generated for the invite.
