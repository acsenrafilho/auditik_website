Our story
2019
- tuvis was founded in Israel under the name Whatslly.
2021
- In November 2021, tuvis obtained seed funding of US$11 million from renowned investors, including Zeev Ventures and Base Partners, who have also financed companies such as Zoom, Nubank, Outreach, Houzz and Audible.
2022
- tuvis undergoes a brand redesign, changing its name from Whatslly to tuvis, inspired by the words “visibility” and “empowerment”.
2023
- tuvis signs a partnership with Inovabra Habitat, Bradesco’s startup incubator.
- We include Compliance and Security solutions in our portfolio.
- We joined Cubo Itaú, guaranteeing networking, mentoring, access to investors and visibility.
2024
- We were selected, among 3 thousand companies, to participate in Scale-Up Endeavor 2024, an acceleration program for the fastest growing companies in Brazil. A very important step for the company’s growth.
Present in more than 60 countries
Tuvis spans more than 60 countries, offering secure and compliant instant messaging solutions for businesses. Our global presence guarantees protection in communications on unofficial channels, such as WhatsApp and iMessage.
