Coralogix
The integration between Tuvis and Coralogix forwards messages exchanged on corporate WhatsApp, DLP (Data Loss Prevention) incidents, and platform audit logs directly to Coralogix, where they are analyzed in real time alongside the organization’s other observability data.
As a result, security and operations teams gain complete visibility into corporate messaging events within the same platform used to monitor application, infrastructure, and security logs — without needing to consult separate tools to investigate communication incidents.
Benefits for your company
• Archiving of messages, media, and metadata from WhatsApp, Telegram, and other channels in Coralogix for compliance and regulatory supervision.
• Forwarding of audit logs from the Tuvis platform to Coralogix for real-time analysis, correlation, and monitoring.
• Automatic sending of DLP incidents to Coralogix with classification by severity, channel, and involved user.
Key features
• Complete observability of communications: messaging events are sent to Coralogix, eliminating operational blind spots.
• Centralized visibility: messaging audit logs and DLP incidents are available in the same observability environment used by security and operations teams.
• Faster detection and response: automatic alerts based on Tuvis events reduce the time needed to identify and respond to messaging security incidents.
Centralize messaging logs and incidents in Coralogix — and maintain full visibility across every corporate channel.
