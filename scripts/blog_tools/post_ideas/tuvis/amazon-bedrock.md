Amazon Bedrock
The integration between Tuvis and Amazon Bedrock enables VIS — Tuvis’s artificial intelligence — to access a catalog of generative AI models such as Claude, Amazon Nova, and Llama. It analyzes corporate messages exchanged via WhatsApp, Telegram, and other business communication channels.
It is also possible to enable DLP (Data Loss Protection) within AI panel interactions to identify and block the sharing of sensitive information in real time.
Key Features
- VIS (Tuvis AI) connected to Amazon Bedrock, providing access to multiple generative AI models without relying on a single provider.
- Flexible switching between available Bedrock models depending on the use case.
- DLP activation to identify and block sensitive content across integrated channels and VIS interactions.
Benefits for Your Company
- AI without data exposure: VIS ensures that messages processed through Amazon Bedrock remain within a controlled environment, with no risk of leakage to public models.
- Productivity powered by intelligence: advanced search, summaries, translation, and message classification make corporate communications faster and more efficient.
- AI-enabled compliance: enable DLP so VIS can automatically detect potential data leakage risks in conversations in real time.
Use generative AI in corporate communications with the security and governance that only Tuvis provides — powered by Amazon Bedrock.
