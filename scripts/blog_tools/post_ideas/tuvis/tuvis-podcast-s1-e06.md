Woman in tech, humanization and changes in consumer behaviour (Feat. Salesforce)
Join us on the tuvis Podcast as we dive into the dynamic world of consumer behavior and celebrate the remarkable achievements of women in the technology industry. In this episode, we have the honor of welcoming Thais Padilha, VP Solution Engineer at Salesforce, as our special guest. She shares her journey and serves as a shining example of success in the field of technology, inspiring listeners to pursue their own aspirations.
