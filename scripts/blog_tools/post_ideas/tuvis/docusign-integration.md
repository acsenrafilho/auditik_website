APPS AND INTEGRATIONS
DocuSign integration
DocuSign eSignature is the world’s #1 way to sign electronically on practically any device, from almost anywhere, at any time. View envelope status, send reminders, sign documents, and start collecting signatures directly from WhatsApp.
Send documents for e-signature directly on WhatsApp
On the Tuvis’ panel, you can see the list of templates from DocuSign and select the one you need to send to your contact. After selecting the right template, you just need to fill in the information and send the document. All without leaving the WhatsApp screen.
Check all the envelope statuses directly on WhatsApp
On the Tuvis’ panel, you can check what is the current status of the envelope/document you sent to a customer or lead. So you can know exactly who opened, who declined, who still needs to sign, and who already signed.
