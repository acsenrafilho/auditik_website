Palo Alto
The integration between Tuvis and Palo Alto Networks leverages the App-ID technology of the Next-Generation Firewall (NGFW) to identify and block access to WhatsApp and other messaging channels through web browsers, redirecting usage to Tuvis Desktop. The granularity of Palo Alto Networks NGFW enables application-specific policies, blocking browser-based messaging access without affecting other network operations.
Key Features
- Granular blocking of WhatsApp Web and messaging channels using Palo Alto Networks App-ID.
- Application-level control that blocks browser access without impacting other organizational network activities.
- Access allowed exclusively through Tuvis Desktop within the firewall security perimeter.
Benefits for Your Company
- Precise application control: Palo Alto Networks App-ID accurately identifies and blocks browser-based messaging channels without creating network disruptions.
- Traffic visibility: The NGFW logs all access to messaging channels, providing valuable data for audits and incident investigations.
- Next-generation messaging security: WhatsApp and Telegram channels are protected with the same level of security applied to other critical business applications.
