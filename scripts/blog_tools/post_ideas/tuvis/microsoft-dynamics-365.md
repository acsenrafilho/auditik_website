Microsoft Dynamics 365
The integration between Tuvis and Microsoft Dynamics 365 connects corporate messages exchanged via WhatsApp, Telegram, and other supported channels directly to the CRM, automatically logging every interaction within Contacts, Leads, Accounts, and Opportunities. This integration consolidates instant messaging communications within the same Microsoft environment that sales and customer service teams use daily.
Benefits for your company
Messaging App Usage: Securely enable WhatsApp and Telegram with automatic conversation logging in Dynamics 365.
Increased Productivity: The integrated CRM panel facilitates activity and task logging on the same screen, without overwhelming sales or support teams.
Full History: Access all conversations within the Dynamics 365 Timeline, providing the team with more context and focus during interactions.
Native Microsoft 365 Integration: Messages recorded in Dynamics 365 connect with Teams, Outlook, and SharePoint, unifying communication across the Microsoft ecosystem.
Key features
Unified view of customer/lead data directly from Dynamics within the Tuvis CRM panel, without leaving the WhatsApp or Telegram screen.
Automatic logging of messages, media, and metadata in the Dynamics 365 Timeline.
Messaging data stored for queries, reporting, and integration with the Microsoft Power Platform.
