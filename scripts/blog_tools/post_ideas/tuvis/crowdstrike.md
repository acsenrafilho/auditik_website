CrowdStrike Falcon
The integration between Tuvis and CrowdStrike Falcon leverages Falcon’s device control and prevention policies to block access to WhatsApp Web and other messaging channels directly on the corporate endpoint, redirecting usage to Tuvis Desktop. Unlike network-layer restrictions, endpoint-based controls ensure that the policy remains enforced regardless of the network or location being used.
Key Features
- Block WhatsApp Web and messaging channels directly on endpoints through CrowdStrike Falcon policies.
- Device-based enforcement that remains active regardless of network connection or user location.
- Allow access exclusively through Tuvis Desktop, with full traceability through the Falcon console.
Benefits for your company
- Endpoint-level control: Restrictions apply directly to devices, whether employees are working in the office, remotely, or on external networks.
- Consistent protection: Network changes, VPNs, or external connections cannot bypass restrictions because policies are enforced at the endpoint level.
- Unified visibility: Messaging channel access is logged within the CrowdStrike Falcon console alongside other endpoint security events.
