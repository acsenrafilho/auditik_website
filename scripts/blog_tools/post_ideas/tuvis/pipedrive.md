Pipedrive
The integration between Tuvis and Pipedrive automatically records all messages exchanged via WhatsApp, Telegram, and other supported channels directly into Pipedrive CRM Deals, Contacts, and Organizations, creating a complete audit trail of every sales interaction. This integration eliminates manual activity logging and ensures that every messaging conversation is captured as a Pipedrive Activity, keeping the pipeline always up to date and salespeople focused on closing deals.
Benefits for your company
Messaging Apps Enabled and Recorded: Communicate through the most popular apps while interactions are automatically saved in Pipedrive.
Real-Time Pipeline: Logged conversations update Pipedrive, providing real visibility into negotiations.
Goodbye to Manual Logging: Salespeople stop updating the CRM; history is filled in automatically.
Data-Driven Strategy: Analyze messaging volume, frequency, and usage (by salesperson, team, and stage) to optimize sales performance.
Key features
Unified view of customer/lead data directly from Pipedrive within the Tuvis CRM panel, without leaving the WhatsApp or Telegram screen.
Automatic logging of messages, media, and metadata in Pipedrive CRM Deals, Contacts, and Organizations.
Linking of conversations with full interaction history visibility per negotiation directly within the Pipedrive dashboard.
