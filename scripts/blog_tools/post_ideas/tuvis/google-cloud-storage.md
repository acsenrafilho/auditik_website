Google Cloud Storage
The integration between Tuvis and Google Cloud Storage (GCS) centralizes all corporate messages exchanged via WhatsApp, Telegram, and other corporate channels into GCS buckets. Designed for organizations operating on Google Cloud, this integration connects messaging data directly to the other analytical tools within the ecosystem.
Activity logs and DLP (Data Loss Prevention) incidents are automatically stored with full traceability and event correlation, transforming GCS into the company’s central repository for corporate communications.
Benefits for your company
Messaging App Usage: Securely enable WhatsApp, Telegram, and other apps with data stored in the GCP ecosystem your team already operates.
Message Analysis: Query archived messages using standard SQL directly in GCS via BigQuery, without moving data between systems.
Immutable Storage for Compliance: Ensure that records are not altered or deleted before the end of the regulatory retention period.
Intelligent Cost Management: Automatically migrate older messages to archival tiers, drastically reducing the total cost of communication storage on GCP.
Incident Detection: Google Cloud’s native SIEM correlates Tuvis DLP incidents with other security events within the GCP environment.
Key features
Storage of messages, media, and metadata in GCS buckets with encryption and access control.
Protection to prevent messaging data leaks outside the security perimeter defined by the company.
Forwarding of DLP incidents with auditable and exportable records for compliance analysis.
Archive every corporate conversation in Google Cloud — and transform your messaging data into business intelligence.
