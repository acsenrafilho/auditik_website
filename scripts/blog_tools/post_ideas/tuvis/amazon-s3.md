Amazon S3
Benefits for your company
Messaging app usage: Enable the corporate use of WhatsApp and other apps with the assurance that every interaction will be automatically archived.
Message analysis: Amazon services can easily and directly query S3 objects without the need to move or transform data for analysis.
Scalability: S3 scales automatically as message volume grows, with no need to provision capacity.
Immutable retention: Compliance mode prevents the alteration or deletion of records before the retention period ends, meeting LGPD, SOX, and FINRA requirements.
Cost optimization: With S3 Lifecycle Policies, older messages are migrated to lower-cost storage options, drastically reducing expenses
Key features
- Automatic storage of messages, media, and metadata in S3 buckets.
- Protection to prevent messaging data leaks outside the company’s defined security perimeter.
- Forwarding of DLP incidents with full traceability.
