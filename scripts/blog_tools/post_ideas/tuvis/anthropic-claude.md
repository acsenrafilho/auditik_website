Anthropic (Claude)
The integration between Tuvis and Anthropic connects VIS — Tuvis’s artificial intelligence — to Claude models, recognized for their superior ability to understand context and generate natural language. This enables VIS to analyze long conversation histories, generate accurate summaries, and suggest responses directly within the platform.
It is also possible to enable DLP (Data Loss Protection) to monitor and protect sensitive information shared across integrated channels.
Key Features
- VIS (Tuvis AI) connected to Anthropic models for high-precision message analysis and summarization with advanced natural language understanding across multiple languages.
- Draft generation and response suggestions directly within the Tuvis platform, based on conversation history analyzed by Claude.
- DLP activation to identify and block sensitive content across integrated channels.
Benefits for Your Company
- AI without data exposure: VIS ensures that messages processed by Claude remain within a controlled environment, protecting sensitive corporate information.
- Advanced conversation understanding: Claude processes and interprets messages with high natural language accuracy, making search and communication analysis far more efficient.
- AI-enabled compliance: enable DLP so VIS can automatically detect potential data leakage risks in conversations in real time.
