Forcepoint
The integration between Tuvis and Forcepoint Web Security applies advanced content filtering and traffic inspection policies to restrict access to WhatsApp and other messaging channels in corporate browsers. Access is granted exclusively through Tuvis, ensuring that the organization’s Acceptable Use Policy extends to instant messaging without creating security exceptions.
Key Features
- Restriction of access to WhatsApp Web and other messaging channels through Forcepoint Web Security filtering policies.
- Access granted exclusively through Tuvis Desktop, within the organization’s controlled perimeter.
- Integration with existing Forcepoint filtering categories, without the need to create policies from scratch.
Benefits for Your Company
- Messaging policy enforcement: Forcepoint also controls access to WhatsApp, Telegram, and similar channels.
- No messaging shadow IT: employees cannot bypass controls using standard web browsers.
- Enhanced governance: all corporate messaging communications take place within Tuvis’s auditable environment.
Extend Forcepoint DLP protection to every corporate message — without making exceptions for instant messaging channels.
