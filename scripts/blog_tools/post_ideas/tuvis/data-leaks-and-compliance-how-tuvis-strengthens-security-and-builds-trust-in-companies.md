Compliance as a Strategic Response to Data Leaks
Compliance serves as a framework for prevention, control, and response to risks, including those related to data protection. Regulations such as Brazil’s LGPD and the European GDPR establish clear guidelines for collecting, processing, and storing data. Failing to comply with these laws can result in heavy fines, legal action, and irreversible damage to brand reputation.
Implementing a culture of compliance is not just about ticking boxes, it means embedding processes that promote ethics, transparency, and integrity across operations. When a data breach occurs, it’s this governance ecosystem that enables a fast, coordinated, and legally sound response.
