Tuvis has just taken another important step toward complete protection of corporate communications. Our new integration with Signal is now available — and ready to transform the way your company manages digital conversations.
How the tuvis and Signal integration works
With this new integration, companies can monitor and archive all interactions made via Signal, including text messages, images, videos, and documents shared on the platform. All of this is done transparently, without affecting the end-user experience.
Additionally, the collected data can be sent directly to your CRM or preferred database, ensuring all interactions are recorded in real time and available for audits, compliance, or productivity analysis.
Check out the 5 key benefits this integration brings to your company:
1. Guaranteed compliance
With increasingly strict regulations (such as LGPD, GDPR, FINRA, among others), ensuring proper communication archiving is essential. tuvis enables all Signal interactions to be securely stored and accessed.
2. End-to-end security
The combination of Signal’s encryption and tuvis monitoring capabilities ensures full visibility of messages without compromising the privacy of data exchanged through the app.
3. Integration with your CRM or compliance system
Records are automatically stored in the tools your team already uses, like your CRM, streamlining processes and eliminating the risk of data loss.
This means your team can access the entire message history and reconnect with clients without repeating already answered questions, for example.
4. Centralized control of multiple channels
Alongside WhatsApp, iMessage, and Telegram, Signal is now part of the portfolio of monitored channels by tuvis, providing a unified view of all your organization’s digital communications.
5. Incident monitoring
Track in real time what’s being communicated, set policies, and receive alerts about incidents occurring on the channel.
With tuvis, managers can create rules to prevent certain words from being sent, for instance — and when such a rule is violated, they’re instantly alerted.
As digital channels become an essential part of business operations, having solutions that keep pace with this evolution — responsibly — is critical. At tuvis, we continue to innovate to deliver real-time compliance with the best experience for your team.
