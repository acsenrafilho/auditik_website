The way consumers shop and interact with brands has changed dramatically in recent years. Increasingly connected, they research reviews, compare prices and seek support before making a purchase decision. In this scenario, providing effective virtual customer service is not only a competitive advantage, but a necessity to increase sales and customer loyalty.
The power of virtual customer service
Companies that invest in technology to improve the customer experience gain not only agility, but also operational efficiency. According to a survey by Opinion Box and Dito, 86 per cent of consumers believe that technology makes the buying process easier. In addition, 72% expect a personalised service that recognises their individual needs.
In this context, digital service channels such as WhatsApp and integrated CRM platforms have become essential for managing the entire customer journey, from first contact to after-sales service. According to a study by RD Station, 70 percent of Brazilian companies already use WhatsApp to attract and retain customers.
Strategies to boost sales with virtual customer service:
- Personalised service Consumers want to be recognised and served in a unique way. Integrating platforms such as WhatsApp into CRM gives the team access to a complete history of interactions, enabling a more agile and personalised service.
- Exclusive offers and promotions Offering discounts and promotions via virtual customer service can be an excellent strategy for attracting and retaining customers. Not only does this approach increase sales, it also demonstrates attention to the needs of the customer, increasing their satisfaction.
- Agile and efficient support Quick responses make all the difference to the customer experience. Tools such as tuvis’ solutions help companies archive messages in real time and optimize support, ensuring more efficient and secure communication.
- Building lasting relationships Virtual customer service goes beyond solving problems: it should be seen as an opportunity to build a solid relationship with the consumer. Well-structured support, from first contact through to after-sales service, contributes to brand loyalty and a positive reputation.
Invest in virtual customer service and improve your results
As consumer habits become increasingly digital, investing in technology to improve virtual customer service is essential. With secure and intelligent solutions such as those from tuvis, your company can strengthen its digital presence, improve the customer experience and enhance its reputation.
