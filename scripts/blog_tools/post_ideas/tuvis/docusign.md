DocuSign
The integration between Tuvis and DocuSign allows your team to send documents for electronic signature and track envelope status directly from the Tuvis dashboard, without leaving WhatsApp. From document delivery to signature confirmation, the entire signing process happens within the same customer interaction and negotiation workflow, eliminating the need to switch between platforms.
Key Features
- Send documents for electronic signature through DocuSign directly from the Tuvis dashboard, without leaving WhatsApp.
- Track envelope status in real time — sent, viewed, or signed.
- Send reminders and resend pending signature requests directly from the Tuvis dashboard.
Benefits for Your Company
- Messaging-first workflows: enable WhatsApp for negotiations while formalizing contracts with electronic signatures in the same workflow, without leaving the dashboard.
- Faster deal closures: send documents for signature at the right stage of the negotiation and monitor progress without switching between tools.
- Full visibility into the signing process: know exactly where each document stands — sent, viewed, or signed — directly within the Tuvis CRM dashboard.
