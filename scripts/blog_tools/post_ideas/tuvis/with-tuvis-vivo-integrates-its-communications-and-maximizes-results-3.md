The Challenge
In Brazil’s dynamic telecommunications market, process efficiency and digitalization were crucial to business success. Recognizing the strategic importance of this area as a market leader in the country, the telecommunications company sought an innovative solution to transform its communications, offering customers agile and personalized service. The partnership with Tuvis was the turning point toward a new era of efficiency and exceptional results.
The company was already using WhatsApp as its main communication channel with customers, but faced obstacles related to integration, archiving, and tracking of interactions, as well as the absence of message history, which hampered monitoring, compliance, and long-term customer relationships.
The Solution
With the implementation of Tuvis, the telecommunications company found the ideal solution to its communication challenges. The intuitive, integrated tool provided a 360° view of interactions, delivering valuable insights for all teams. Message transcription simplified monitoring and ensured records were preserved, meeting compliance and security requirements.
Results
The implementation of Tuvis delivered operational benefits, improved cross-team collaboration, and reduced costs by replacing multiple tools with a single integrated solution.
“Tuvis significantly improved our daily operations by providing a user-friendly interface and easy access to relevant customer information. The ability to generate active contacts on WhatsApp increased our retention and engagement, resulting in a significant boost in overall performance.”
— Marketing Manager
The partnership between the telecommunications company and Tuvis transformed the company’s communications, enabling more efficient and personalized customer interactions. The team was then able to focus on strategic activities, drive sales, and deliver a smoother customer experience. With Tuvis as a partner, the telecommunications company integrated its communications and maximized its results in a competitive and constantly evolving market.
