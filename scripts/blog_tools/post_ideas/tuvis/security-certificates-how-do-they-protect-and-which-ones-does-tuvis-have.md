Which certificates does Tuvis have?
In the digital environment, certificates represent the virtual identity of a company, providing guarantees and security in various transactions. They allow the company to carry out transactions and establish contacts in a protected manner, guaranteeing the integrity of the company’s activities.
tuvis has two certifications that guarantee compliance with security standards and reliable practices, reinforcing its commitment to protection and transparency for all parties involved. See below what they are:
ISO/IEC 27001
This certification is the most important international standard for information security management. It establishes guidelines for identifying, assessing and managing risks associated with data control, ensuring the protection of information and the integrity of a company’s technological systems.
ISO/IEC 27001 certification demonstrates that the organization has adopted international best practice for information security, ensuring the confidentiality, integrity and availability of data.
SOC 2 Type II
SOC 2 Type II certification guarantees that an organization has implemented strong security, privacy and availability controls to protect sensitive data. Designed for technology companies and cloud service providers, it demonstrates the effectiveness of these controls over time.
To achieve this certification, an external auditor assesses whether the organization meets the AICPA’s criteria. Although it is not a legal requirement, many companies apply for this seal to guarantee the reliability and security of their partners and suppliers.
