How to change your sales using tech (Feat. Microsoft)
Three super women host the first tuvis Podcast! Deborah Wanzo, President and Co-founder of tuvis, Andrea Cerqueira, VP of Sales at Microsoft, and Patricia Travassos from CNN, discuss how technology is transforming sales, the new possibilities in the industry, and also address the role of women in the market.
