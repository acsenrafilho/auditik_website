The Tuvis integration with Outlook Calendar allows you to have a bigger picture of your overall schedule without leaving WhatsApp. With a few clicks, you can connect your Outlook account through the Tuvis panel and view all your tasks, meetings, or events on any day of the month.
Automatic syncing with Outlook Calendar
You will be able to see all your meetings and events from your Outlook calendar without leaving the WhatsApp Web screen. Stay updated and informed on your schedule while chatting with customers through WhatsApp.
Schedule meetings
Without leaving the WhatsApp screen, you will be able to schedule a meeting or event in your Outlook Calendar. All in a few clicks.
