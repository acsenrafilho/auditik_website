About dogs, cats, birds and business (Feat. Petlove)
According to the 2021 Census, Brazil ranks third in the world for having the highest number of pets. It is estimated that the pet market has generated a revenue of R$ 59.2 billion! To discuss this loving relationship between pet owners and their pets, as well as the significant business opportunities in this market, we have invited Hugo Rodrigues, CEO B2B and Services at PetLove, the largest ecosystem for animals in Brazil, for our conversation. Where there are pets, there is love, and there are also plenty of business opportunities!
