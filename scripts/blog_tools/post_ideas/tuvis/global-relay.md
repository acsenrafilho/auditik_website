Global Relay
The integration between Tuvis and Global Relay connects corporate messages exchanged via WhatsApp, Telegram, and other supported channels to the Global Relay platform for supervision, eDiscovery, and regulatory auditing.
Activity logs and DLP (Data Loss Prevention) incidents detected by Tuvis are also sent to Global Relay, ensuring that all messaging events are centralized in a compliance-ready repository designed to meet regulatory requirements such as SEC, FINRA, FCA, and LGPD.
Benefits for your company
• Immutable archiving of messages, media, and metadata from WhatsApp, Telegram, and other channels in Global Relay to meet regulatory requirements.
• Forwarding of DLP (Data Loss Prevention) incidents and audit logs from Tuvis to Global Relay with auditable records and preserved chain of custody.
• Support for eDiscovery and regulatory investigations with search and export of archived messages directly within the Global Relay platform.
• Application of configurable retention policies by data type, channel, and regulatory jurisdiction.
Key features
• Enable messaging applications: allow the use of WhatsApp and Telegram in the corporate environment with every conversation archived and available for regulatory supervision in Global Relay.
• Built-in regulatory compliance: messages captured by Tuvis are delivered to Global Relay ready to meet requirements from SEC, FINRA, FCA, MiFID II, and LGPD.
• Fast eDiscovery: locate and export archived messages in seconds to support regulatory investigations and legal processes.
• Immutable retention: records archived in Global Relay are protected against alteration or deletion, ensuring full integrity for audits
