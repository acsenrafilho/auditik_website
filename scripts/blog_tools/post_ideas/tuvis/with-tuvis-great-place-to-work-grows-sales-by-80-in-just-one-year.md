Transforming companies into great places to work is the mission of Workplace Consultancy, which for 25 years has been a global authority in the job market. Present in more than 90 countries, the consultancy believes in the importance of building healthy and inclusive work environments for all.
The Challenge
Excellence starts at home: serving 10,000+ companies requires highly personalized and attentive communication.
The Solution
To deliver more agility and personalization, Workplace Consultancy invested in tuvis technology. The goal: to transform daily customer interactions while also improving new business prospecting.
By integrating consultants’ individual WhatsApp accounts with Salesforce, the company automated manual processes that previously consumed valuable time. This brought more speed, efficiency, and a delightful experience for clients.
In just one year of partnership with tuvis, each consultant saved around one hour per day — a huge impact given the thousands of employees in the organization. Time once spent on operational tasks is now dedicated to strategic activities such as follow-ups, customer conversations, and product demos.
With fewer manual tasks, consultants focused more on sales, driving an 80% increase in revenue in just one year.
With accurate data and insights provided by tuvis, managers can now go beyond just listing prospects: they understand them in depth, making decision-making more strategic and effective.
All on a single screen. With a single click. This is tuvis.
