Google Meet
The integration between Tuvis and Google Meet connects corporate messaging workflows directly to Google Workspace. Meetings scheduled through the Tuvis CRM dashboard are automatically created in the user’s Google Calendar, and invitations are sent to contacts without needing to open Google Calendar separately or manually generate meeting links. For teams already working within the Google ecosystem, the integration eliminates constant context switching between tools.
Key Features
- Schedule Google Meet meetings directly from the Tuvis CRM dashboard, with automatic event creation in Google Calendar and invitation delivery to contacts.
- Automatic synchronization with the user’s Google Calendar, keeping the Google Workspace schedule always up to date.
- Start Google Meet video calls with contacts without leaving the Tuvis dashboard.
Benefits for Your Company
- Google Workspace integrated into the sales workflow: events created from the dashboard automatically appear in Google Calendar with no extra steps required.
- Less duplicate work: no need to open Google Calendar after arranging a meeting through WhatsApp — everything happens in one place.
- Unified experience for Google ecosystem users: messaging, scheduling, and video meetings connected through the Tuvis dashboard.
