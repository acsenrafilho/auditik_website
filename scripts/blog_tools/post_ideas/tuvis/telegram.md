Telegram
Telegram is increasingly becoming a key communication channel in the corporate environment, primarily due to its groups, channels, and fast file-sharing capabilities. However, without proper monitoring and archiving mechanisms, it can lead to a lack of visibility, security risks, and compliance gaps.
Tuvis transforms Telegram into a secure and auditable corporate channel by capturing and protecting all company interactions. Our solution ensures complete communication archiving, real-time supervision, and alignment with global regulations, including LGPD, FINRA, SEC, and GDPR.
