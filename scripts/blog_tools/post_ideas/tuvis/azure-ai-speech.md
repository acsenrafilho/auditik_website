Azure AI Speech
The integration between Tuvis and Azure AI Speech closes a common gap in corporate compliance policies: voice messages. Audio files exchanged through WhatsApp and other supported channels are automatically archived and transcribed, with the content indexed within the Microsoft ecosystem — making it searchable, auditable, and eligible for DLP (Data Loss Prevention) policies just like any other message managed by Tuvis.
Key Features
- Automatic archiving of audio files exchanged through WhatsApp and other channels, with secure and traceable storage.
- High-accuracy audio transcription powered by Azure AI Speech, with multilingual support and indexed content for search and auditing.
- DLP (Data Loss Prevention) activation over transcribed text for identifying sensitive content in voice messages.
Benefits for Your Company
- Complete governance over voice communications: audio messages receive the same compliance treatment as text messages — archived, transcribed, and fully auditable.
- Search and investigation across audio content: transcribed messages are indexed and searchable, allowing teams to locate information in voice messages without manually listening to every file.
- Sensitive data protection in audio communications: DLP applied to transcriptions automatically identifies restricted content shared through voice messages, closing a common security gap in messaging policies.
