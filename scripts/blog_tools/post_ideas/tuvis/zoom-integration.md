APPS AND INTEGRATIONS
Zoom Integration
Zoom is now available one click away from WhatsApp chats with your favorite tools and features on a single screen. Tuvis instantly integrates the powerful features of Zoom into WhatsApp Web, providing easy and direct access to day-to-day tasks without leaving the instant messaging screen.
Initiate instant Zoom video calls with individuals or groups
When integrating your Zoom account into WhatsApp, you will be able to initiate a Zoom meeting and automatically send the link of the meeting to your contact without leaving WhatsApp.
Schedule Zoom meetings with contacts
When scheduling a meeting through the Tuvis’ panel on WhatsApp, you will be able to select Zoom, and the link to the Zoom meeting will be automatically generated for the invite.
