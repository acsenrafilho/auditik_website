Proofpoint
The integration between Tuvis and Proofpoint connects corporate messages exchanged via WhatsApp, Telegram, and other supported channels to Proofpoint’s compliance and archiving platform, ensuring that all communications are captured, stored immutably, and made available for supervision, eDiscovery, and regulatory auditing.
Activity logs and DLP (Data Loss Prevention) incidents detected by Tuvis are automatically forwarded to Proofpoint, centralizing all corporate messaging events within the same environment used to govern emails and other digital communications across the organization.
Key Features
• Forwarding of DLP incidents and audit logs from Tuvis to Proofpoint with auditable records and preserved chain of custody.
• Configurable retention policies by data type, channel, and jurisdiction, aligned with the organization’s regulatory requirements.
Benefits for your company
• Enable messaging applications: allow the use of WhatsApp and Telegram with every conversation archived and available for supervision and auditing in Proofpoint.
• Unified communications governance: WhatsApp and Telegram messages are delivered to Proofpoint alongside emails and other communications, eliminating compliance silos.
• Simplified eDiscovery: legal and compliance teams can quickly locate and export archived messages without relying on manual processes or external tools.
• Immutable retention for compliance: records protected against alteration or deletion ensure full integrity of evidence for audits and investigations.
