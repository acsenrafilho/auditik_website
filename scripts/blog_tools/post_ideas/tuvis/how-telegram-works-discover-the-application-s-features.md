With technological advances, messaging applications have become key players in the lives of thousands of people and companies. Among them is Telegram, which offers functions for exchanging data and sending instant messages.
Launched in 2013, the app allows users to connect and interact in different ways, sharing photos, videos, audio and documents stored in the cloud. With the expansion of the digital world, Telegram already has more than 900 million monthly active users and is used not only for personal purposes, but also for strategic services for companies and entrepreneurs.
How Telegram stands out for businesses
Although Telegram is not the most popular messaging channel, its multi-functionality has fuelled its growth. In 2023 alone, there were 22 million new downloads, according to the Demandsage research institute. As well as being a channel for interaction, Telegram facilitates the dissemination of campaigns and news, making it an essential pillar for strengthening relationships between businesses and consumers.
With various communication options, users can create individual chats, groups with up to 200,000 members or broadcast channels for promotions and commercial announcements, for example. These widely used tools offer an effective strategy for companies wishing to reach different audiences and keep their consumers up to date.
Telegram also allows you to create private channels, accessible only through a link, for those interested in specific topics, and secret encrypted chats, ideal for exchanging confidential information, such as sensitive requests from large companies. To facilitate the exchange of large files, the app allows you to send and download documents of up to 2 GB without restriction – such as high-quality videos, presentations, film footage, institutional books and much more.
Telegram stands out as an effective platform for strengthening the online presence of large companies looking to improve internal communications and relationships with customers and investors. When combined with security technologies, the messaging channel becomes even more strategic and reliable for the exchange of information.
