Technology used to prevent leaks of confidential information, with automatic blocking and message monitoring. Protects sensitive data from being leaked either by mistake or intentionally.
With Tuvis proprietary DLP module, your company can enforce policies and custom rules to monitor communications in real time. Track keywords and specific rules to prevent data leaks. Learn more about this and other security solutions.
Tuvis integrates DLP policies into channels like WhatsApp and SMS.
