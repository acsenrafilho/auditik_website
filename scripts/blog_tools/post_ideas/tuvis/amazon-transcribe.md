Amazon Transcribe
The integration between Tuvis and Amazon Transcribe transforms voice messages into searchable and auditable records. Audio files exchanged through WhatsApp and other supported channels are automatically archived and transcribed with speaker identification and custom vocabulary tailored to the company’s business context — improving transcription accuracy for technical terms and industry-specific corporate jargon. The content is securely stored and indexed within the AWS ecosystem, ready for auditing and DLP (Data Loss Prevention) enforcement.
Key Features
- Audio transcription powered by Amazon Transcribe, with multilingual support, speaker identification, and custom vocabulary, plus indexed content for search and auditing.
- Automatic archiving of WhatsApp audio files and calls, along with their corresponding transcriptions.
- DLP (Data Loss Prevention) activation over transcribed text for identifying sensitive content in voice communications.
Benefits for Your Company
- Complete governance over voice communications: audio messages receive the same compliance treatment as text messages — archived, transcribed, and fully auditable within the AWS ecosystem.
- Context-aware accuracy: Amazon Transcribe’s custom vocabulary capabilities improve transcription precision for technical terminology and business-specific corporate jargon.
- Sensitive data protection in audio communications: automatically identifies restricted content shared through voice messages, closing a common gap in messaging security policies.
