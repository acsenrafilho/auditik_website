APPS AND INTEGRATIONS
Google Meet Integration
Google Meet is now available one click away from WhatsApp chats with your favorite tools and features on a single screen. Tuvis instantly integrates the powerful features of Google Meet into WhatsApp Web, providing easy and direct access to day-to-day tasks without leaving the instant messaging screen.
Initiate instant Google Meet phone and video calls with individuals or groups
When integrating your Google Meet account into WhatsApp, you will be able to initiate a Google Meet meeting and automatically send the link of the meeting to your contact without leaving WhatsApp.
Schedule Google Meet with contacts
When scheduling a meeting through the Tuvis’ panel on WhatsApp, you will be able to select Google Meet, and the link to the Google Meet meeting will be automatically generated for the invite.
