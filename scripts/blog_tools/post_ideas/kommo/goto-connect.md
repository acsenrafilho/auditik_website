The GoTo Connect integration turns Kommo into a connected calling workspace. With GoTo, you can place and receive calls directly from lead cards, while every interaction is automatically logged in the CRM. Smart workflows take care of follow-ups and reminders, so your team spends less time on manual tasks and more time building relationships. This integration brings all your voice communication into one streamlined hub — fast, reliable, and fully in sync with your sales process.
Enhance your calling workflow and never miss an opportunity:
-
Click-to-call functionality: Initiate calls directly from lead cards with a single click, eliminating the need to dial manually.
-
Automatic call logging: Keep a complete record of call details, including duration and outcome, within each lead card.
-
Call reminders: Set up automated reminders for follow-up calls, ensuring timely communication with leads.
-
Faster follow-ups: Trigger automated tasks after calls, such as scheduling a meeting or sending an email.
Kommo clients use GoTo Connect because it eliminates the busywork of making calls. One-click dialing replaces manual entry of numbers, call logs appear automatically, and reminders handle follow-ups. Add in seamless call routing, and teams save hours while delivering a smoother customer experience.
To connect GoTo Connect, follow these steps:
- Go to Settings > Integrations > Search GoTo Connect and click Install.
- Enter your GoTo Connect login credentials.
- Authorize the connection and select your extension numbers.
That’s it, your connection is live ⭐. Visit our setup guide to learn more about this connection.
