The ActiveCampaign integration in Kommo enables seamless synchronization between your CRM and email marketing platform. By connecting ActiveCampaign, you can automate email campaigns, synchronize contacts, and enhance lead engagement directly within Kommo. This integration streamlines your marketing efforts, ensuring timely and personalized communication with your leads.
Elevate your email marketing strategy with these innovative uses:
-
Automated email campaigns: Trigger personalized email sequences based on lead behavior and pipeline stages.
-
Contact synchronization: Keep your lead and contact information up-to-date across both platforms, ensuring consistency.
-
Segmented lists: Create targeted email lists in ActiveCampaign based on lead attributes and interactions captured in Kommo.
-
Behavioral triggers: Set up automated responses to lead actions, such as opening an email or clicking a link, to nurture relationships.
-
Performance tracking: Monitor the effectiveness of your email campaigns by analyzing open rates, click-through rates, and conversions within Kommo.
Boost your email marketing with Kommo’s ActiveCampaign integration. Leverage Kommo’s advanced features to personalize campaigns, automate workflows, and engage leads smarter. Sync contacts, track every interaction, and get a complete view of each lead’s journey - all from within Kommo’s powerful CRM, turning email marketing into a revenue-driving engine.
To connect ActiveCampaign, follow these steps:
-
Go to Settings > Integrations > Search ActiveCampaign and click Install.
-
Enter your ActiveCampaign API URL and API Key.
- Configure your synchronization settings and preferences.
That’s it, your connection is live ⭐. Visit our setup guide to learn more about this connection.
