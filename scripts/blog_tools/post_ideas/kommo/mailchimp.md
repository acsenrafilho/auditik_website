The Mailchimp integration lets you add new features in your account:
- Subscribe leads to email campaigns in one click
- Update email lists in bulk
- Automatically subscribe leads to campaigns based on triggers (Advanced plan)
Mailchimp is a leading email marketing platform which helps you build beautifully branded email campaigns. It features an easy and intuitive email builder with many customizations and automation of your campaigns.
Kommo provides Mailchimp users with a complete set of sales and marketing tools. Create an all-around sales & marketing automated workflow with unprecedented messenger based features.
By combining Kommo and Mailchimp you can track all of your leads and move them across the sales process while being able to easily add Kommo leads to your Mailchimp campaign. All it takes is one click. If you want to save extra time, just let Kommo do all the work to automatically subscribe contacts to your Mailchimp campaigns when they reach specific stages of the pipeline.
The Mailchimp integration is built-in and designed by the Kommo team. Installing it is simple and takes only a few steps. Click on Install in Settings > Integrations and authorize the connection.
