Always start with a spreadsheet. “Think of it as your sketchpad. It will help you develop a clear plan before building anything.” -
In your spreadsheet, identify the stages: “What is point A, and what is point B? What must be done to get from the first point to the second one?” Point out the differences between the stages: “Every stage needs to be different and have a certain call to action, and you need to understand what needs to be accomplished in every stage.” Start building your communication chain: “Ask yourself, what kind of messages will the reader receive at every stage? What do I want people to do at this stage?”
