The Calendly integration allows new meetings to automatically create leads or tasks in Kommo. If a contact already exists, a meeting task is added to their lead card. Advanced automation in the Digital Pipeline enables unique scheduling links, automatic lead stage changes, and syncing with the Kommo Calendar for complete visibility.
Use this integration to automate scheduling and capture every lead effortlessly:
-
Automatic lead creation: New meetings scheduled through Calendly generate new leads in Kommo automatically.
-
Meeting task creation: If a contact already exists, a task is added to the lead card for the scheduled meeting.
-
Calendar syncing: All meetings are synchronized with the Kommo Calendar for a full overview of events.
-
Pipeline automation: Advanced users can create Digital Pipeline automations to move leads to the next stage after meetings are scheduled.
-
Meeting outcomes: Add notes and outcomes directly in the lead card during or after the meeting.
Kommo users love Calendly because it eliminates the back-and-forth of scheduling and automatically logs meetings in the CRM. This ensures no lead is missed, keeps all meeting details in one place, and enables timely, personalized follow-ups for stronger customer engagement.
- Go to Settings > Integrations > search Calendly.
- Click Install on the Calendly widget.
- Enter your Calendly API key to connect the accounts.
- Select Calendly as a lead source in your pipeline.
- Configure meeting tasks, lead creation, and pipeline automations as needed.
