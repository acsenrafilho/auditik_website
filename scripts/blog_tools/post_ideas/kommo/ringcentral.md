The RingCentral integration in Kommo transforms your CRM into a comprehensive calling solution. By connecting RingCentral, you can make and receive calls directly from lead cards, log all call activities, and automate call-related workflows. This integration streamlines your communication processes, ensuring that every call is efficiently managed and tracked within your CRM.
Enhance your calling workflow and never miss an opportunity:
-
Click-to-call functionality: Initiate calls directly from lead cards with a single click, eliminating the need to dial manually.
-
Automatic call logging: Keep a complete record of call details, including duration and outcome, within each lead card.
-
Call reminders: Set up automated reminders for follow-up calls, ensuring timely communication with leads.
-
Centralized communication: Access all call history alongside your other connected channels and tasks in Kommo, giving you the full customer picture in one place.
The RingCentral integration empowers Kommo users to manage all voice communication directly within the CRM. With features like click-to-call, automatic logging, and call reminders, users can engage leads effectively and efficiently. The seamless integration ensures that all interactions are logged and accessible, providing a comprehensive view of each lead's journey. This enhances productivity, improves customer engagement, and streamlines communication processes.
To connect RingCentral, follow these steps:
- Go to Settings > Integrations > Search RingCentral and click Install.
- Enter your RingCentral login credentials.
- Authorize the connection and select your extension numbers.
That’s it, your connection is live ⭐. Visit our setup guide to learn more about this connection.
