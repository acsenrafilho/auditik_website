Integrating Kommo into WordPress elevates your website to a higher pedestal. Kommo turns WordPress into a powerful tool to interact with your clients in real time easily.
WordPress lets people build powerful websites to place their products or brands online so potential clients can access them easily.
Kommo lets WordPress users create a chat button on their websites, allowing them to communicate with clients from different channels in one inbox easily.
This integration lets you display the chat button on your website. By default, the button is displayed in the lower right corner of the browser window.
When you click the button, a chat window opens, allowing your clients to send messages through multiple channels. These messages will be sent automatically to your Kommo inbox.
Kommo lets you connect all your messaging channels to your website so you and your clients always stay in contact. Even on the go, you can receive messages on your mobile device and respond immediately, ensuring no lead gets lost.
Additionally, you can use Kommo’s salesbot to automate all communication processes. Relax and let the bot do the job!
