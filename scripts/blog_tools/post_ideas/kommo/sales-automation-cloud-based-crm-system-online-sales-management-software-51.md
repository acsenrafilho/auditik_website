Google Sheets is a cloud-based spreadsheet tool from Google that lets you create, edit, and share spreadsheets in real time. With this integration, you can automatically turn new entries into leads. It's a simple way to collect form data, survey results, or prospect lists and send them directly into your pipeline – no manual copy-pasting needed.
Use cases
-
Turn form submissions into leads – If you collect contact details through a Google Form, the linked sheet can push those responses into Kommo as new leads, letting your team act on them right away.

-
Manage prospect lists from marketing campaigns – When running email or ad campaigns, you can log interested contacts in a shared Google Sheet. The integration will create a lead in Kommo for every new row added, so your sales team can start follow-ups instantly.
-
Sync partner or reseller referrals – If external partners or resellers submit leads through a shared sheet, Kommo can automatically pull those entries into your pipeline without needing manual input.
-
Collect offline leads – At events or trade shows, your team can add leads to a shared sheet. As long as the integration is active, those leads will appear in Kommo and can be distributed or qualified right away.
