The Typeform integration in Kommo enables seamless lead capture by automatically converting form responses into leads within your CRM. By connecting Typeform, you can streamline your sales pipeline, ensuring that every form submission is promptly followed up on and managed efficiently. This integration enhances your lead management process, reducing manual data entry and accelerating your sales cycle.
Enhance your lead management and sales processes with these creative uses:
-
Instant lead creation: Automatically create leads in Kommo whenever a Typeform is submitted, ensuring no lead is missed.
-
Custom field mapping: Map Typeform responses to specific lead fields in Kommo, capturing all relevant information.
-
Automated pipeline entry: Direct new leads into a designated pipeline stage, streamlining your sales workflow.
-
Real-time notifications: Receive instant notifications in Kommo when a new lead is created from a Typeform submission.
-
Enhanced lead segmentation: Use form responses to segment leads based on their interests or needs, enabling targeted follow-ups.
Kommo users appreciate the Typeform integration because it automates the lead capture process, reducing manual data entry and ensuring timely follow-ups. By seamlessly connecting Typeform with Kommo, users can maintain an organized and efficient sales pipeline, leading to improved lead conversion rates and enhanced customer engagement.
To connect Typeform, follow these steps:
- Go to Settings > Integrations > Search for Typeform and click Install.
- Sign in to your Typeform account and authorize the connection.
- Choose the form you want to integrate with Kommo.
- Map the form fields to the corresponding lead fields in Kommo.
That’s it, your connection is live ⭐. Visit our setup guide to learn more about this connection.
