JustCall empowers your team to go from rep to #SuperRep. It logs all calls and text messages under the contacts and companies – making it easier for sales reps to keep a track of all communication in a single place.
Financial and insurance, Real estate, Education
Make, Receive and Track Calls – With JustCall, you can make, receive, record and track all your phone calls right from Kommo. Get all your call activities automatically logged and get access to call recordings and analytics. Send and Receive Text Messages – You can send and receive text messages to the contacts/companies. All inbound and outbound text messages get logged under the contact/company automatically. Detailed Call Analytics & Live Call Monitoring – Get access to all your call related analytics, recordings, voicemails and missed calls.
