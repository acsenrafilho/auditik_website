The CloudTalk integration in Kommo transforms your CRM into a comprehensive calling solution. By connecting CloudTalk, you can make and receive calls directly from lead cards, log all call activities, and automate call-related workflows. This integration streamlines your communication processes, ensuring that every call is efficiently managed and tracked within your CRM.
Enhance your calling workflow and never miss an opportunity:
-
Click-to-call functionality: Initiate calls directly from lead cards with a single click, eliminating the need to dial manually.
-
Automatic call logging: Keep a complete record of call details, including duration and outcome, within each lead card.
-
Call reminders: Set up automated reminders for follow-up calls, ensuring timely communication with leads.
-
Call routing: Seamlessly route calls to the appropriate team member, improving response times and customer satisfaction.
CloudTalk stands out for Kommo users because it makes calling effortless. With CloudTalk, sales teams don’t waste time switching tools; everything happens inside Kommo. Calls are crystal-clear, every conversation is automatically logged, and follow-ups never slip through the cracks. Users say it feels like having a personal call assistant built into their CRM, helping them focus on what matters most: building relationships and closing sales.
To connect CloudTalk, follow these steps:
- Go to Settings > Integrations > Search CloudTalk and click Install.
- Enter your CloudTalk login credentials.
- Authorize the connection and select your extension numbers.
That’s it, your connection is live ⭐. Visit our setup guide to learn more about this connection.
