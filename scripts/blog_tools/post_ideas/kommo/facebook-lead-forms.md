The Facebook Lead Forms integration in Kommo enables you to automatically capture leads from Facebook lead ads and import them directly into your sales pipeline. This integration ensures that every lead is promptly added to the “Incoming Leads” stage, complete with pre-populated contact information, allowing your sales team to engage with qualified prospects without delay.
Use this integration to enhance your lead management and communication:
-
Automated lead capture: Automatically create leads in Kommo when a Facebook lead form is submitted, ensuring no prospect is missed.
-
Pre-populated contact information: Leverage Facebook's autofill feature to gather accurate contact details, reducing manual data entry.
-
Custom field mapping: Map Facebook form fields to corresponding fields in Kommo to maintain data consistency and relevance.
-
Pipeline automation: Utilize Kommo's automation features to move leads through the sales pipeline stages based on predefined criteria.
-
Integrated communication: Engage with leads directly within Kommo using integrated messaging tools, streamlining communication and follow-ups.
The Facebook Lead Forms integration provides Kommo users with a seamless method to capture and manage leads from Facebook ads. By automating lead entry and utilizing pre-populated contact information, sales teams can focus on engaging with qualified prospects. Combined with Kommo's robust automation and messaging features, this integration enhances efficiency and accelerates the sales process.
To set up the Facebook Lead Forms integration in Kommo:
- Navigate to Leads > click on Automate.
- Click on Add source and select Facebook > Lead Ads Form. (If you haven’t signed in yet, you’ll be prompted to do so)
- Choose the desired Facebook Business Page and select the lead form you wish to integrate.
- Map the fields from the Facebook lead form to the corresponding fields in Kommo.
- Once connected, new leads from the selected form will appear in the “Incoming Leads” stage of your pipeline.
