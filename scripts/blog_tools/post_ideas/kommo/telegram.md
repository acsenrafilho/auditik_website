The Telegram integration in Kommo enables you to manage conversations, create leads from incoming chats, and use automation to provide round-the-clock service. With this connection, Telegram becomes a direct channel inside your CRM, ensuring no message or opportunity is missed.
Use this integration to simplify communication and boost customer engagement:
-
Lead creation: Automatically generate new leads whenever someone messages you on Telegram.
-
Centralized chat: Reply to Telegram messages directly inside Kommo’s lead card.
-
Chat history: Keep all conversations saved and accessible within your CRM.
-
Automation: Deploy Salesbot to answer FAQs, collect customer data, and send invoices.
-
Customer feedback: Send forms at the end of a chat to track customer satisfaction.
The Telegram integration gives Kommo users a secure and fast way to stay connected with customers. Paired with Kommo’s visual pipelines, Salesbot, and automation features, it extends Telegram beyond simple messaging - turning it into a 24/7 business channel that captures leads, engages prospects, and keeps communication structured inside Kommo.
The Telegram integration is built in and designed by the Kommo team. To connect it:
- Create a bot in Telegram.
- Copy the bot’s API token.
- Paste the API token in Kommo.
- Start chatting directly from your lead cards.
That’s it, your connection is live ⭐. Visit our setup guide to learn more about this connection.
