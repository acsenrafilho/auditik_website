The Zoom integration adds new features to your account:
- Easily set up webinars, audio and video conferencing with leads and customers
-
Find the New meeting button in the lead card and send an invitation to a Zoom meeting via email or messenger
- Store automatically every Zoom video and call recordings in the lead card, making it easy for you to access all past meetings
Kommo users love Zoom as a fantastic addition to their arsenal of sales tools that allows you to make a video call with every lead. It only takes a few clicks to set up and run an online meeting your leads will love.
Zoom users love Kommo to easily store and view their meetings on their devices in one click. It’s super easy for you to access all past meetings and even download them whether you’re using Kommo on your smartphone, tablet or laptop.
The new integration allows you to make a video call with every lead. Now you can find the New meeting button in the lead card and send the invitation to a Zoom meeting either via email or messenger. It only takes a few clicks to set up and run an online meeting your leads will love.
Now you can automate your sales process with Zoom + Kommo’s Salesbot by automatically creating video meetings and sending the invitation link to your lead after they fill out a form or move to a specific stage - let the Salesbot handle it.
To connect your Zoom account in Kommo, go to Settings → Integrations and choose the Zoom integration. Then simply press Install and sign in to your Zoom account
