Custom presets allow you to automatically map out all of the fields, this way you won’t have to manually select fields every time you want to create a new lead. This is especially useful if you often use a specific website to look for new lead contact details.
Instructions on how to create a preset can be found in the extension.
Creating a preset yourself will let you grab LinkedIn profile info and populate Kommo lead card fields based on your specific business needs.
We have created 2 custom presets for LinkedIn which you could use straight away. One can be used for LinkedIn profiles and the other for job applicants on your listing. Download LinkedIn presets.
