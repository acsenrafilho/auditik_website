The RD Station integration in Kommo allows you to connect your marketing automation platform directly with your CRM. Automatically capture leads from landing pages, synchronize their data, and trigger marketing workflows, all while keeping your sales pipeline organized. This integration ensures that no leads are lost and marketing actions feed directly into your sales process.
Kommo users love the RD Station integration because it makes managing leads effortless. With automated import, data synchronization, and task triggering, sales teams spend less time on administrative work and more time closing deals. Combined with Kommo’s pipelines, messaging, and automation, this integration ensures that leads move smoothly from marketing to sales without any friction.
To connect RD Station, follow these steps:
-
Go to Settings > Integrations > Find RD Station and click Install.
-
Sign in to your RD Station account and authorize the connection.
-
Map RD Station form fields to Kommo lead card fields.
That’s it, your connection is live ⭐. Visit our setup guide to learn more about this connection.
